Territory:

Service Provider agrees to territory exclusivity within the Client's specific niche and geographic market for the duration of this Agreement. Service Provider will not onboard a direct competitor operating in the same niche and territory. Maximum 3 coaches per territory, ever. Territory lock takes effect on the Effective Date.

5. Term of Agreement

This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues on a rolling basis for the selected billing cycle. Following the initial billing period, the Agreement auto-renews unless either party provides written notice of termination in accordance with Section 11 of this Agreement.

6. Performance Guarantee

Service Provider guarantees that by Month 3 of this Agreement, Client's content will be consistently converting followers into enquiries, with a measurable content system fully operational across their selected platforms. Specifically: Month 1 — content pillar and brand voice fully built and live; Month 2 — consistent posting cadence established, audience engagement growing; Month 3 — content converting clients, warm followers entering the enquiry pipeline.

If these milestones are not met by Month 3: Service Provider will refund the most recent month's service fee within 7 business days of written request, provided Client has fulfilled all responsibilities in Section 7. "Converting" is defined as followers taking measurable action (DM, link click, form fill) as a result of content posted under this Agreement.
7. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to:

  • Complete onboarding questionnaire and brand discovery within 5 business days of signing.
  • Provide access to brand assets, login credentials, and approvals within 48 business hours of request.
  • Maintain the recommended ad spend budget on designated ad platforms throughout the engagement.
  • Respond to booked discovery call leads within the timeframes established during onboarding.
  • Designate a primary point of contact available to respond within 48 business hours.
  • Not interfere with or duplicate outreach campaigns running under this Agreement.
  • Provide honest and complete information about your coaching niche, audience, and outcomes.
8. Results Disclaimer

Beyond the performance guarantee outlined in Section 6, the Service Provider makes no additional warranties regarding specific revenue, lead volume beyond the guaranteed threshold, or conversion results. Marketing outcomes depend on factors including but not limited to market conditions, offer strength, client responsiveness, and platform algorithm performance.

9. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary information, strategies, client data, pricing, and business processes disclosed in connection with this Agreement. This obligation survives termination for a period of two (2) years.

10. Intellectual Property

All creative assets, captions, content strategies, hooks, and frameworks developed by Service Provider remain the intellectual property of Service Provider until full payment is received. Upon payment for each billing period, deliverables created for that period become Client's property. Proprietary systems, templates, and frameworks remain the exclusive property of Service Provider.

11. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice after the initial billing period. Termination does not relieve Client of obligation to pay for services already rendered. Service Provider may terminate immediately if Client engages in fraudulent, abusive, or unethical conduct, or fails to make payment after 14 days past due.

12. Limitation of Liability

In no event shall Service Provider be liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Service Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid by Client in the three (3) months preceding the claim.

13. Dispute Resolution

The parties agree to first attempt resolution through good-faith negotiation. If unresolved within 30 days, disputes shall be submitted to binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

14. Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, representations, or agreements. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.
